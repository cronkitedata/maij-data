Visualization as a reporting tool

Visualizing data is a whole ’nother skill, but we can borrow from the design and statistics world to help make our reporting more efficient. Don’t worry about style here – think about whether you’ll be able to “see” a story easier in infographic form than in numbers.
Published

March 14, 2022

From now on, you won’t have a lot of homework - most of your work will be either in-class labs or projects on your own, so you don’t have a lot of preparation for any classes. I’ll start listing in the agenda links to any presentations or other materials we use in class.
